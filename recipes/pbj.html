PBJ Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread

  • Peanut Butter

  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place to slices of bread flat on a plate.

  2. Spread Peanut butter on a slice.

  3. Spread Jelly on the other slice of Bread.

  4. Place the slices of bread together in a manner which the Peanut Butter and Jelly are making contact with each other.

  5. Your PBJ is complete enjoy.
